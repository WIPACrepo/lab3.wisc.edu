Exhibit

    The LAB3 Exhibit: Collaborative artwork, interdisciplinary conversations

       

    24 high school students, 12 artists and writers, and 6 physicists.

    A series of art installations at the intersection between science and the arts.

    Opening reception: Sep 8, 2018, 6-9 pm, featuring live performances and short presentations of the creative process.

    Gallery hours: Wed–Sat, 11am to 3 pm, thru Sep 29, 2018.

    Address: ARTS + LITERATURE LABORATORY
    2021 Winnebago Street
    Madison, WI 53704

     

     

     

    Get a free ride to the exhibit!

    Free guided visits to the exhibit with complimentary transportation for Madison middle schools. Two days only: Thu Sep 13 & Wed Sep 19. Book your visit here.

    To schedule other group visits, contact the ALL gallery at hello at artlitlab.org.

    LAB3 has as its main goal the promotion of conversations among scientists, writers, artists, and students, which will connect scientific thinking and discoveries with artistic research and experimentation.

     

    WIPAC

    WIPAC is a UW–Madison research center focused on neutrino astrophysics and the headquarters of the IceCube Neutrino Observatory.

    ALL

      ALL is a non-profit organization that encourages visual, literary, and performing artists to explore and develop their craft, and provides a supportive and challenging environment in which ideas can be shared.
